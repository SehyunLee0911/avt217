Blue Jazz Club

Navigation

Personally I think it has a very good navigation system. Buttons have a nice allignment also having the exact information. Great platform for both PC and mobile.

Usability

Any user can use this site without strugling to find buttons and read the explanation for any contents. Does not have long loading terms with clear interfaces.

Typography and hierarchy

Typography is great, using specific fonts for specific information, has different color of fonts to distinguish information. Ex) "Buy Tickets", "Appearing Tonight", and description of the artist's show.

Overall style/look and feel

Personally I would say it fits well on a Jazz Club, because it looks fancy and simple at the same time with restricted fonts and colors, that makes the mood.
