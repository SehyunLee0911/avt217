FAQs

Q: Are the customers allowed to enter later than the show's scheduled time?

A: It's possible, however for the smooth progress of the show, we recommend the customers to arrive 30 minutes before the start.

Q: How long are the parking lots available for?

A: Customers who plan to park on one of the parking lots above will be able to park until the show finishes.

Q: What is the difference between the premium ticket and the normal ticket?

A: Premium tickets will lead you to spots, so customers can not only enjoy drinks and food. Normal tickets only leads to the bar, with standing as mandatory.
